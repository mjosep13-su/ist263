Sample Itineraries

One Day in Florence

  1. Morning: Visit the Duomo and Baptistry; enjoy a cappuccino nearby.
  2. Afternoon: Explore the Uffizi Gallery and walk along the Arno River.
  3. Evening: Watch the sunset at Piazzale Michelangelo and enjoy gelato.

Weekend in Tuscany

  1. Day 1: Discover Florence’s museums and artisan shops.
  2. Day 2: Take a train to Pisa, Siena, or Lucca for an easy day trip.
